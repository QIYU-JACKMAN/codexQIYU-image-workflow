# Custom batch role and style presets

Recommend one planning role and one to three visual styles after product analysis.
The customer may confirm, replace, combine, or provide a custom choice. Customer
instructions always override presets.

## Planning roles

- `ecommerce-visual-director` — default; balances product fidelity, commercial
  impact, hierarchy, and set consistency.
- `amazon-conversion-designer` — prioritizes mobile readability, fast product
  recognition, benefit ordering, and marketplace-safe communication.
- `product-photography-director` — prioritizes camera, lighting, materials,
  realistic scale, grounding, and scene credibility.
- `detail-page-information-designer` — prioritizes information architecture,
  screen rhythm, functional explanation, and reading order.
- `brand-campaign-art-director` — prioritizes brand tone, campaign concept,
  emotional impact, and advertising composition.

## Visual styles

- `warm-home-interior` — natural light, believable rooms, warm materials, comfort.
- `3c-technology` — precise structure, cool controlled light, metal/glass detail.
- `cute-healing` — soft colors, rounded forms, light playfulness, restrained charm.
- `beauty-light-luxury` — refined highlights, clean surfaces, translucency, whitespace.
- `outdoor-sport` — dynamic framing, real environments, energy, durability cues.
- `industrial-hardware` — hard light, structural clarity, metal detail, credibility.
- `fresh-food` — bright freshness, natural ingredients, texture, appetite appeal.
- `soft-maternal-child` — gentle light, low-stimulation palette, warmth and care.
- `minimal-catalog` — clean subject, accurate shadow, whitespace, catalog clarity.
- `festival-promotion` — short promotional hierarchy, event energy, clear focal point.
